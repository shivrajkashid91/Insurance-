FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about insurance, answered simply.

What is the difference between term insurance and life insurance? +
Term insurance provides pure life cover for a specific period (10–40 years). If the policyholder passes away during this term, the nominee receives the sum assured. Traditional life insurance (endowment/whole life) combines insurance with savings. Term plans offer higher cover at lower premiums, making them ideal for income protection.
How does cashless hospitalization work? +
Under cashless hospitalization, you can get treated at any of our 10,000+ network hospitals without paying upfront. The insurer settles the bill directly with the hospital. You only need to show your health card and submit a pre-authorization request. Your out-of-pocket expense is zero for covered treatments.
How do I file an insurance claim? +
Filing a claim is simple: contact us via WhatsApp, phone, or email, and our dedicated claim support team will guide you through every step. For health claims, we provide a claim form and hospital documents checklist. Motor claims are initiated by calling the helpline within 24 hours of the incident. Most claims are processed within 24–72 hours.
Can I port my existing health insurance to a better plan? +
Yes! IRDA allows health insurance portability. You can switch to a better insurer without losing your accumulated no-claim bonus or waiting period credits. The process must be initiated 45 days before your renewal date. Our advisors handle the entire portability process for free.
What factors affect my insurance premium? +
For life/health insurance: age, sum assured, medical history, lifestyle habits, and coverage type. For motor insurance: vehicle age, make/model, IDV, location, and add-ons. Our advisors analyze all these factors to find the most cost-effective plan for your profile across 20+ insurance companies.
Is my personal data safe with SecureLife? +
Absolutely. We follow strict data privacy protocols under the IT Act and IRDA guidelines. Your information is used solely to find the best insurance plans for you and is never sold or shared with unauthorized parties. All our digital systems use 256-bit SSL encryption.
